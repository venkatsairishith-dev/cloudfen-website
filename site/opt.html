Optional Practical Training

OPT is temporary employment authorization that lets F-1 students work in their field of study for up to 12 months.

Home/Careers/OPT

Optional Practical Training (OPT) is temporary employment authorization for F-1 international students, directly related to their major area of study.

Key points

Eligible F-1 students may apply for up to 12 months of OPT, used before completing studies (pre-completion) or after (post-completion).

  • Employment must be directly related to the student's major.
  • Authorized by USCIS through an Employment Authorization Document (EAD).
  • Up to 12 months total per education level.
  • STEM graduates may apply for a 24-month extension (STEM OPT).

How CloudFen helps

CloudFen provides relevant, field-aligned opportunities for students on OPT and supports a smooth onboarding process.
