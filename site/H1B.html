H-1B Visa

The H-1B is a US work visa for specialty occupations that require specialised knowledge and at least a bachelor’s degree or its equivalent.

Home/Careers/H-1B

The H-1B is a non-immigrant visa that allows US employers to hire foreign professionals in specialty occupations — roles that require specialised knowledge and typically a bachelor’s degree or higher.

Who it is for

The H-1B is designed for specialty occupations across fields such as IT, engineering, finance and the sciences. The role must require the theoretical and practical application of specialised knowledge.
